Privacy Policy

This extension ("Kick Theme Styler") does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

The extension only modifies the visual appearance of the website Kick.com based on user-defined preferences, such as background color, font color, or logo replacement. These preferences are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's local storage API.

No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties. We do not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking mechanisms.
